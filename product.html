PRODUCTS


Papaya


Scientific name of papaya is "Carica papaya". It native to tropical America, from Southern Mexico through the Andes of South America. Papaya is a fruit with a green skin, sweet yellow flesh, and small black seeds. Papaya also has sweet taste, vibrant color, and the wide variety of health benefits they provide make them a popular fruit.

Coconut


Scientific name of coconut is "Cocos nucifera". This fruit likely was first cultivated on islands in Southeast Asia, meaning the Philippines, Malaysia, Indonesia, and perhaps the continent as well. Coconut is a round and elongate fruit of white, fibrous and oily meat covered by a thick, brown and hairy shell.

Durian


Scientific name of durian is "Durio". This fruit from the region of Borneo and Sumatra, they grew wild along the Malay peninsula, and was commonly cultivated in a wide region from India to New Guinea. Durian has a thick fibrous husk, covered by sharp spines or thorns. The flesh or pulp is creamy, white, yellow or golden yellow.

Banana


Scientific name of banana is "Musa acuminate". This fruit originally found in South East Asia, mainly in India. Banana is a curved, yellow fruit with a thick yellow skin and soft sweet flesh. It is one of the world's most appealing fruits. Most of people likes eat banana. People can create a lot of recipe use banana.

Dragon Fruit


Scientific name of dragon fruit is Hylocereus undatus. The origin of this fruit is unknown, but it is probably native to Central America. This fruit comes in four varieties; three are pink-skinned, one with white flesh, one with red flesh, and the third with purple flesh.

Watermelon


Scientific name of watermelon is Citrullus lanatus. First watermelons appeared in South Africa some 5,000 years ago so the origin of this fruit is South Africa. It has a sweet, juicy, refreshing flesh of yellowish or reddish colour, containing multiple black, brown or white pips.

Rambutan


Scientific name of rambutan is Nephelium lappaceum. It native to Malaysia and commonly cultivated throughout the archipelago and southeast Asia. Rambutan is round or ellipsoid with a leathery skin densely covered in soft spines up to 2 cm long. The fruits are yellow to crimson and grow up to 7 by 5 cm in size.

Manggo


Scientific name of manggo is Mangifera indica. It native to southern Asia, especially Burma and eastern India and It spread early on to Malaya, eastern Asia and eastern Africa. Manggo are 2 to 9 inches long and may be kidney shaped, ovate or round. The flesh is soft and bright yellow-orange in colour.
